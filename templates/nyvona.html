    About ForraNova Hub

    ForraNova Hub is a high-performance Neural Research and Academic Ecosystem. It merges scientific research, AI creative synthesis, and sophisticated academic testing into a single "Neural Sync" interface.

    Philosophy: Information should not just be stored, but synchronized with the mind. Using a Neural Vault (IndexedDB), the app creates a "living memory" of every document, calculation, and creative project.

    Features Architecture

    • 🧠 Academy Core: AI chat with RAG, quizzes, exams, and progress tracking
    • 📚 RAG Engine: Upload textbooks for retrieval-augmented generation
    • 📖 Reader: Text summarization, key point extraction, read-aloud
    • 📊 Analytics: Performance charts and mastery tracking
    • 📓 Digital Trove: Save and organize notes locally

    Academy Guide

    Setting Your Focus: Use the dropdowns to pick your subject and level.

    AI Chat: Ask questions. The AI will respond based on your selected subject, level, and any uploaded RAG files.

    Quizzes: Test your knowledge with AI-generated questions.

    Exams: Multi-phase exams with objectives, identification, and theory.

    Resource Lab

    RAG Upload: Drag and drop textbooks (PDF, TXT, MD) to enhance AI responses with your own materials.

    Reader Tools: Summarize text, extract key points, or have it read aloud.

    Voice Input: Use the mic button for voice-to-text questions.
